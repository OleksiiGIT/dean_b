vision

Classic, sophisticated perfection with square shape. Dean Brochard has got the best combination of timeless beauty together with incredible uniqueness of square form. Choose between a classic mix of roman numbers with smooth lines or go for slim stripes. Either way, feel pure and classic experience dressed up in squared glamour.

quality

High quality is something that we at Dean Brochard value highly. Dean Brochard carefully select only the best materials and finest designs for our products and we regularly visit our factories to maintain a high standard as a watch brand. We want to give you a simple, fun and rewarding experience for all purposes by visiting our website, stores or purchasing our products.

Warranty

3 years warrenty from the date of the purchase will be offered, so that you can feel secure when purchasing from our watch shop online or in store, so that you can focus on living life to the fullest deluxe. The Warranty covers manufacturing defects on the watch itself (movement, case, crystal).

payment & delivery

Secure payment at all times when purchasing from Dean Brochard. We accept different payment options as Klarna Invoice and cards like Visa, Mastercard, American Express and other local payments. When purchasing watches online from Dean Brochard you always have free shipping and free returns worldwide!
