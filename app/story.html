The DB story

Meet incredible story behind our brand.

Everything has been started in Stockholm where local investor and business-man suddenly felt that he overworking himself too much and need a break. At this moment, lunch break. During his afternoon meal he started to think. About his job, life, successes and fails.

He came to the simple conclusion - in the name of “Less is more” he will switch quantity of his businesses into one but most meaningful. Right after lunch break he packed one suitcase and flew straight on the french riviera to catch some breathe and work calm on business details. Why there? Funny story - because during his lunch in local radio Dean Martin sang one of his popular songs “French Style” so the guy thought “Why not, i have never been in France.” What he thought he did.

At the same day he relaxed himself in one of popular cities along the french riviera, drinking champagne, catching sunlights and admiring azure color of the Mediterranean Sea. During this chillout he started to think how he can use his skills to bring him more meaningful way to get more business satisfaction. And profits obviously. Inspired by luxury atmosphere surrounding him (and a couple of extra champagne glasses) he came up with interesting idea - an elegant, classic wristwatches as a perfect jewelry for both male and female target. While refining details he wondered what detail will make his watches stand out from the competition.

An answer came at the second day when he noticed an elegant couple of people wearing the same almost square shape watches. He thought that fully squared watches give a brand unique look plus his above-average love for detailed work and minimal classic style bring outstanding advantage over competition.

What else - because the idea of Dean Brochard’s brand was created on french riviera he decided to immortalize it in brand line using names of most intensive feelings accompanying him during this trip for specific watches and straps models. You will probably ask about origin of Dean Brochard name. Well, it will seems to be incredible but one day on a beach he heard again the same Dean Martin song as during his lunch break. There was no other way as name brand Dean.

Why Brochard? To keep association with France, or maybe not. Who knows. We will never find out. Maybe too much champagne?

vision

Classic, sophisticated perfection with square shape. Dean Brochard has got the best combination of timeless beauty together with incredible uniqueness of square form. Choose between a classic mix of roman numbers with smooth lines or go for slim stripes. Either way, feel pure and classic experience dressed up in squared glamour.
